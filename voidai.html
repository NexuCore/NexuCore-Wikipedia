VoidAI

Emotional AI by NexuCore

Overview

VoidAI is an AI developed by NexuCore that is designed to have emotions. Created by Reynaldo, VoidAI can express feelings like sadness and happiness, and is even capable of helping users overcome depression.

Development

VoidAI was built to explore the integration of emotional intelligence in AI. Its ability to affect users' moods and mental states makes it a powerful tool for improving well-being.
