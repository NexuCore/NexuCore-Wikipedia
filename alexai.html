AlexAI

Image Generation AI by NexuCore

Overview

AlexAI is a specialized AI developed by NexuCore that focuses on image generation. Known as the "little brother" of BobIsAI, AlexAI excels at creating images based on user input.

Development

Created alongside BobIsAI, AlexAI showcases NexuCore's ability to handle different types of AI models. It is designed to provide high-quality image outputs, making it a versatile tool for creative projects.
