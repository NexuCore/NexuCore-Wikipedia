Mehmet Inanç

Artist & Creator of PenguinBot at NexuCore

Overview

Mehmet Inanç is an artist at NexuCore and the creator of PenguinBot, a smart and cool AI penguin that excels at engaging in chill chats with users.

Role in NexuCore

Mehmet was responsible for the design and conceptualization of PenguinBot, bringing creativity and personality to the AI, making it an engaging and enjoyable companion for users.
