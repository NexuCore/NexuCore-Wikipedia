BobIsAI

AI developed by NexuCore

Overview

BobIsAI is an advanced AI developed by NexuCore. Named after the creator's old gaming username "BobIsMad," this AI represents the pinnacle of what NexuCore's programming language, NexuCode, can achieve.

Development

BobIsAI was created to showcase the capabilities of NexuCode, a programming language developed by NexuCore. The AI is designed to interact intelligently with users, providing assistance, information, and more.
